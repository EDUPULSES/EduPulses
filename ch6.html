Tissues

Groups of cells with similar structure and function.

Plant Tissues

Meristematic

Dividing tissues. Responsible for growth. (Apical, Lateral, Intercalary).

Permanent

Lost ability to divide.

  • Simple: Parenchyma, Collenchyma, Sclerenchyma
  • Complex: Xylem (Water), Phloem (Food)
